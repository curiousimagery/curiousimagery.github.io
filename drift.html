step 1 — enable
open settings → accessibility → guided access. toggle it on. set a passcode — this is your admin exit code that only you know.
step 2 — configure
still in guided access settings: tap "time limits" and set an alert sound if desired. under "display auto-lock", choose "never" so the screen stays on indefinitely.
step 3 — launch drift
open drift from your home screen. load your media and tap launch to start playback.
step 4 — lock it down
triple-click the side button. the guided access overlay appears. under "options" at the bottom left, disable "side button", "volume buttons" (unless you want physical volume control), "motion", and "software keyboards". tap "start" in the top right.
step 5 — you're locked in
the phone is now a dedicated drift player. the home gesture, control center, notification center, and app switching are all disabled. users can only interact with drift.
to exit
triple-click the side button → enter your passcode → tap "end" in the top left. you're back to normal ios.
tip
enable face id as a secondary unlock method (in guided access settings) so you can exit quickly without typing the passcode.
hiding the home bar
guided access hides the home indicator bar at the bottom of the screen. if it still appears briefly, it will auto-fade after a few seconds of no touch input. for a truly clean display, guided access is the only way to suppress it in a PWA.
what are banks?
banks let you organize clips into switchable groups — e.g. "ambient" vs "glitch" vs "nature". during playback you can cycle between banks to change the mood.
how to set them up
name your files with a bracket prefix or double-dash prefix. examples: [ambient] ocean-drift.mp4 or ambient--ocean-drift.mp4. the text before the delimiter becomes the bank name.
auto-detection
when you add a folder, drift reads the tags and auto-groups clips into banks. clips without a tag go into "main". up to 4 banks are supported.
no tags? no problem
if none of your files have tags, everything goes into a single bank and the bank UI stays hidden. you can always add more clips from a differently-tagged folder later.
switching banks
keyboard: press b. bluetooth controller: press l or r shoulder button. in the grid view, bank tabs appear at the top when multiple banks exist.
